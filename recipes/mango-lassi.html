Mango Lassi

Description

Mango lassi is a popular beverage in India and other parts of the world. It is a sweet and creamy drink that is perfect for quenching your thirst on a hot day. The drink is made by blending ripe mangoes with yogurt, milk, and sugar. The result is a smooth and frothy drink that is perfect for sipping.

Mango lassi is not only delicious, but it is also healthy. The drink is rich in vitamins and minerals, particularly vitamin C and potassium. Yogurt, which is one of the main ingredients in mango lassi, is a great source of protein and calcium, making it a healthy and filling drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the chopped mango, yogurt, milk, sugar, and ground cardamom (if using).
  2. Blend the mixture until it becomes smooth and creamy.
  3. If you want a chilled lassi, add some ice cubes to the blender and blend again.
  4. Pour the lassi into glasses and serve immediately.
