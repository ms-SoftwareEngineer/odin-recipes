Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Paneer Butter Masala Recipe - Learn to make the best restaurant style paneer butter masala at home. Isn't it fun to recreate restaurant style dishes at home? Years ago, I found this best paneer butter masala recipe in one of the cookbooks in a library. I tried it, tweaked it, then made this for years & my entire family got hooked to it. It's creamy, flavorful & tastes super delicious! It goes so well with Butter naan, roti, Chapati, plain basmati rice or Jeera rice.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer
  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chillies
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped for garnishing

Steps

  1. First, chop the onion, tomatoes, and green chillies.
  2. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp of butter and add the chopped onion. Saute the onion until it turns golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger and garlic paste to the pan and saute for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and green chillies to the pan and saute until the tomatoes turn mushy.
  5. Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down. Once it cools down, blend it into a smooth paste.
  6. In the same pan, heat the remaining butter and add the blended tomato-onion mixture.
  7. Add red chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt to the pan. Cook the mixture for 5-6 minutes.
  8. Add the paneer cubes to the pan and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  9. Add the milk and cream to the pan and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  10. Turn off the heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
